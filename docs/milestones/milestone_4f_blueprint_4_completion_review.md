## Evidence Boundary

A pose observation means a pose adapter produced keypoint evidence for a person-like subject at a media-owned frame/time.

It does not mean the subject identity is known, the player action is known, a serve occurred, a hit occurred, a split-step occurred, a biomechanical conclusion is valid, a rally or point exists, or any result was adjudicated.
